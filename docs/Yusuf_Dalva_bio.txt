Yusuf Dalva is a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech. 
He recieved his Bachelors degree at 2020 and M.Sc. degree at 2023, from Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey.
His research interestsinvolve the intersection of computer vision and machine learning, with an emphasis on generative models.
Until today, his previous research involves robustness enhancement of deep neural networks, image-to-image translation models, inversion of pre-trained GANs and 3D texture estimation.
